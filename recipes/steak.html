Steak

Description

Ingredients
  • Steak
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Thyme
Steps
  1. Aplly salt and pepper to the raw steak
  2. Heat a stove top pan to medium high heat
  3. Put the steak in the heated pan and sear for 3 minutes
  4. Flip the steak and sear for another 2 minutes
  5. Add a half stick of butter and the desired amount of garlic cloves and thyme
  6. Baste the steak for another 3 minutes
  7. remove from the pan and let cool for 5 minutes
